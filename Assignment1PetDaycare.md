# Pet Daycare-Meeting Preparation Document

## Objective:

The main objective of this preparation document is to facilitate the
process to understand the existing problems and detailed requirement.
This will help further document the current and future business
processes, and changes required in all areas including process,
technology and people change management.

## Current High level Problem statements

- New Employee onboarding takes longer time, minimum three months

- New client onboarding is complicated process, and it varies based on
  the individual employee.

- New customer finds onboarding flow is difficult to follow.

## Assumptions:

- Current process is not effective.

- There is no standard structure for hiring employees and performing
  onboarding process.

- There is no employee orientation process in place.

- There is no system used to initiate and track the onboarding tasks.

- There is no feedback and rectification process is in place.

## Requirement Breakdown

Based on the interviews, we can break down the requirements into three
major problems which are related to onboarding:

### New Employee Onboarding- Affected Stake Holder: Owner

- Onboarding new employees takes at least 3 months. This duration needs
  to be reduced.

- Create employee orientation process to speed up the onboarding
  process.

- Classify the trainings as mandatory, required and optional. Align the
  mandatory and required trainings as part of effective onboarding.

- Create a system to initiate and track the onboarding tasks.

- Establish standard structure for hiring and onboarding process.

- Refine and speed up the onboarding new employee process to improve the
  owner’s satisfaction.

### New Client Onboarding- Affected Stake Holder: Employee

- Onboarding a new client is complicated, and each employee has their
  own style when processing the new client. Standardize the new client
  onboarding process across all employees to improve the employee
  satisfaction.

- Create client onboarding template to follow the steps across the
  organisation.

- Provide standard application to initiate and track the tasks for
  client onboarding.

- Provide workflow notification when tasks are overdue during
  onboarding.

### New Customer Onboarding experience- Affected Stake Holder: Customer

- Customers are frustrated due to difficulty in following the onboarding
  process. Prepare the seamless process and notify the customer well in
  advance.

- Prepare the instruction guide for every onboarding task and
  communicate.

- Create customer point of contact to enhance the customer experience.

- Follow up and communicate with the customer through friendly email.

- Create a customer feedback system to enhance the customer experience.

## Questions and knowledge to expose

The following list of questions will help understand the current process
and subsequently will help propose the new process and solution for the
current problems.

### Onboarding New Employee:

1.  What are the steps currently involved in existing onboarding new
    employee process?

2.  What was the feedback from employee after their onboarding process?

3.  Is there any process followed to map the skill matrix during the
    interview and as part of onboarding process?

4.  How were the employees mentored with their training program
    regarding time and efficiency to see any changes can be made to the
    training program?

5.  Is there any explicit area where new employee facing difficulties?

6.  Is there any tracking system used to track the employee onboarding
    progress?

    1.  To update the personal, banking and tax information

    2.  Complete the mandatory training and acknowledgement

    3.  Upload all required documentations

### Onboarding New Client:

1.  What is the existing registration process for new client?

2.  What is the workflow involved in intaking the new client?

3.  What does the best practice of onboarding a client look like from
    employee’s point of view?

4.  Is there any area where employee find difficulty in onboarding new
    client?

5.  Is there any system or tracker to monitor the status of the client
    onboarding?

### Onboarding New Customer experience:

1.  Is there any customer onboarding orientation process exists?

2.  Is there any customer relationship employee/manager assigned during
    the onboarding?

3.  What are the forms customer need to fill out for registration?

4.  Are there customers feedback collected?

5.  What are the details of the customer feedback in relation to their
    onboarding experience?
